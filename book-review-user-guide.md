# Book Review Service User Guide

## Overview

Book Review Service is a web application that allows users to browse books and leave reviews.

Users can view book details, filter books, and share their feedback by creating reviews.

---

## Getting Started

To start using the application:

1. Open the application in your browser  
2. Sign in to your account  
3. Navigate to the main page  

---

## Viewing Books

To view available books:

1. Open the **Books** section  
2. Browse the list of books  

Each book contains:

- title  
- author  
- genre  
- rating  

---

## Filtering Books

You can filter books to find relevant results.

To filter books:

1. Open the **Books** section  
2. Use filter options  
3. Select a genre or author  

The list will update automatically based on your selection.

---

## Viewing Book Details

To see detailed information about a book:

1. Click on a book in the list  
2. Open the book details page  

The page displays:

- full book information  
- average rating  
- user reviews  

---

## Creating a Review

To leave a review:

1. Open the book details page  
2. Click **"Add Review"**  
3. Enter your name  
4. Select a rating (1–5)  
5. (Optional) Add a comment  
6. Click **"Submit"**

Your review will be saved and displayed in the list.

---

## Common Issues

### Unable to submit a review

- Ensure all required fields are filled 
- Rating must be between 1 and 5  

---

### Books are not displayed

- Check your internet connection  
- Refresh the page  

---

## Tips

- Use filters to quickly find books  
- Provide clear and useful reviews  
- Check ratings before choosing a book
